Services

These are our services:

We offer a wide variety of technical services, for all your small business needs. Choose from a variety of services below:

AutoCAD Design

Our designers have experience mainly with electrical and architechtural design, but are capable of meeting any of your needs.

If your company falls under one of the following industries, we can help you with all your AutoCAD needs.

  • Architechture
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Interior Design

PDF Conversion

It is all too often that old pdf files cannot be converted into .dwg files directly and require a human touch prior to design. That is where our autoCAD technician comes in.

We can convert any form of image file into usable .dwg files as well. If you require a site plan and don't have any architechtural drawings, we can create site plans simply from google maps' satellite technology.

Web Services

Whether you are creating a new website for your company, or you want to make edits to a current website, our web technicians can help.

Online website generators can be complicated, costly, or seem unprofessional. After a short consulting meeting, we can set up a tailored website with a professional domain names such as .com or .ca. We will handle all the complications and you can focus on running your business.
