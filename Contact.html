Getting a Quote

If you are contacting us to obtain a quote for a project, please include the following information in your message:

  • What industry does your company operate in?
  • What is the scope of the project? (ex. Do you need large collection of sketches digitized, or do you need a site plan designed?)
  • How urgent is the project, and how soon would you like your designs completed?
  • Your contact information and preferred contact time
  • Any other relevant information

We will reply as soon as possible with a quote and a projected finish date.

Change of Details

It is quite possible that after we begin work, certain details surrounding the project may change. Especially in the construction industry, plans can change at a moments notice. Please let us know of these changes as soon as possible so we can see the correct dream come to fruition.

Filing a Complaint

If you are contacting us with regards to a complaint, please let us know what we did wrong and how we can help. We are certain it will not happen, but any mistakes will be corrected as a first priority and free of charge. Also, please include the following information in your complaint:

  • A description of your complaint
  • Project name/number (if applicable)
  • Your contact information and preferred contact time
  • Any other relevant information
